About Montreal Parking Finder

What is Montreal Parking Finder?

Montreal Parking Finder is an application designed to help drivers find free parking spots in Montreal by analyzing the city's complex parking signalization system. The app uses official parking sign data from the City of Montreal's open data portal and integrates it with OpenStreetMap to provide accurate, real-time parking information.

Finding parking in Montreal can be challenging due to the complex system of signs and rules that vary by time, day, and season. Our app simplifies this process by interpreting these rules and showing you exactly where you can park at any given time.

How It Works

The Montreal Parking Finder works through a multi-step process:

  1. Data Collection: We use the City of Montreal's parking signalization dataset, which contains information about all parking signs in the city.
  2. Sign Interpretation: Our advanced parsing algorithm interprets the complex restrictions on each sign, including time periods, days of the week, and seasonal restrictions.
  3. Street Mapping: We connect each sign to its corresponding street segment using OpenStreetMap data to create intervals where parking rules apply.
  4. Time-Based Analysis: The app checks the current date and time against the parsed restrictions to determine if parking is currently allowed on each street segment.
  5. Visualization: Results are displayed on an interactive map with color-coded street segments: green for free parking and red for restricted or paid parking.
Features
  • Real-time parking availability
  • Interactive maps
  • Location-based search
  • Time-aware restrictions
  • Adjustable search radius
  • Address search
  • Detailed restriction information
  • Mobile-friendly interface
Data Sources

The application uses data from the following sources:

  • Montreal Parking Signalization Data
  • OpenStreetMap
Limitations

While we strive to provide accurate information, please note:

  • The app relies on official data which may not reflect recent changes to parking signs
  • Temporary restrictions due to construction, events, or snow removal are not included
  • Always verify parking rules by checking the actual signs on location
  • The application is not responsible for parking tickets or towing fees
